About Us

Exhibit A is IISER TVM’s student-led science magazine and serves as a dynamic platform where curiosity meets creativity. Featuring a mix of articles, interviews, photographs, short stories, think-pieces, artwork and book reviews, Exhibit A brings science out of the lab and into lively conversation. With new editions every 3 months, the magazine aims to make research more accessible, engaging, and relevant. More than just a magazine, Exhibit A is a space for students to express, critique and connect, a testament to the power of scientific inquiry, dialogue and imagination.
