About Anvesha

Anvesha, the annual science festival of the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Thiruvananthapuram, is a vibrant three-day celebration of science and innovation. Held during the Varsha Semester under the esteemed guidance of Honourable Director Prof. J. N. Moorthy, the festival is organized by the dynamic student-run Science and Technology Council (STC), with support from the various science clubs functioning under STC.

Anvesha is committed to promoting scientific curiosity and awareness among students and the general public. The festival features a diverse range of outreach activities, engaging exhibitions, hands-on workshops, and interactive sessions that bridge the gap between scientific research and society. Through a combination of pre-festival and post-festival events, Anvesha provides a holistic platform that encourages the development of essential skills such as communication, teamwork, and scientific reasoning. It serves as a catalyst for inspiration, learning, and the spirit of scientific research.
