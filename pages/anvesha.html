About Anvesha

Anvesha is the flagship annual science and technology festival of IISER Thiruvananthapuram, where brilliant minds come together to showcase innovation, creativity, and scientific excellence.

Our fest brings together students, researchers, and science enthusiasts from across the country to participate in competitions, workshops, exhibitions, and inspiring talks. It's a celebration of the scientific spirit and a platform for young innovators to showcase their talents.
