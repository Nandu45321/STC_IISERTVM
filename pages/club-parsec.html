About Us

Parsec is our institute’s astronomy club, bringing together stargazers, dreamers, and explorers. From stargazing nights and meteor shower watch parties to hands-on telescope sessions and talks by experts, we make the cosmos accessible and exciting for everyone. Whether you’re spotting constellations for the first time or diving deep into astrophysics, Parsec is your launchpad to the universe.
