About Us

Compass is a newly established club under the school of Earth, Environmental, and Sustainability sciences. It's dedicated to raising awareness about the vital field. Through engaging activities such as cleanliness drives, geological walks, and peer discussions, the club aims to foster a deeper understanding of our planet and inspire students to be conscious about their footprint on this earth and to take meaningful action towards a more sustainable future.
