About Us

We aim to promote coding and programming in general, and help people find opportunities to learn and apply such skills. We also foster innovation and keep the campus updated on the latest developments in technology. To foster this tech savvy spirit, we conduct seminars, webinars, alumni discussions, and movie screenings. We also conduct hands-on workshops and summer projects, for people interested in widening their skill sets. The club has recently been revived (November, 2024) after a long period of inactivity.
